Who We Are

Up Dog was founded in 2022 by two friends committed to exploring the joy of movement and mindfulness. Our team has grown steadily since, joined by over 10 experts in yoga and meditation who have lived its transformative power, and are committed to sharing that with you.

What We Offer

Vinyasa

Vinyasa is a style of yoga characterized by stringing postures together so that you move from one to another, seamlessly, using breath.

Yin

Yin yoga is a quiet and contimplative practice that targets the deep connective tissues of the body, and focuses on the flow and release of energy.

Hatha

Haá¹­ha yoga is a branch of yoga which uses physical techniques to preserve and channel the vital force or energy.

Bikram

Bikram, otherwise known as Hot Yoga, is a form of Hatha yoga that is done in a heated room or sauna to increase the overall body tempurature and energy flow.
